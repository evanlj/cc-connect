# Clarification Question Bank

Pick only the highest-impact questions for each round (3-7 recommended).

## A. Goal & Value
1. The single most important outcome: what must be true after delivery?
2. If only one metric can improve, which metric should it be?
3. What business loss happens if this is delayed by one cycle?

## B. Users & Scenarios
1. Who is the primary user, and who is explicitly not the target user?
2. What is the highest-frequency scenario?
3. What edge scenario must still work on day one?

## C. Scope & Boundary
1. What must be included in MVP?
2. What is explicitly excluded from this phase?
3. Are legacy compatibility requirements mandatory?

## D. Quality & Constraints
1. Performance baseline (latency, throughput, startup, memory)?
2. Reliability target (availability, error budget)?
3. Security/compliance constraints?
4. Deadline and immutable milestones?

## E. Delivery & Acceptance
1. What evidence is required to declare completion?
2. Which acceptance cases are hard blockers?
3. Who signs off final acceptance?

## F. Trade-offs
1. If deadline conflicts with scope, what can be cut first?
2. If quality conflicts with speed, where is the minimum acceptable bar?
3. Which risk is acceptable, and which is absolutely not acceptable?
